# Brand

## Confirmed

- Name and domain: `fullbuild.ai`
- Official tagline: **idea → design → engineering → shipped**
- Load-bearing conceit: **Time-Lapse Manufacture**. Scrolling is time; one artifact is manufactured through four states — vapor, blueprint, machine, fused — as the visitor descends. (Supersedes the 2026-07-20 Build Seam test direction; spec: `docs/specs/2026-07-20-manufacture-design.md`.)
- Content law: every public claim must carry real evidence. The site itself is Case Study 00. Unknown work becomes a visibly authored pending state, never an invented client, metric, testimonial, award, or outcome.
- Lifecycle physics: Idea is vapor, Design is blueprint, Engineering is machine, and Shipped is a sealed crate only after production proof exists.
- Palette: ink `#17151F`, milk `#F3F0E8`, cobalt `#3347FF`, orchid `#A987FF`, lemon `#E9FF65`, coral `#FF6B58`, and mineral `#8BE7FF`.
- Typography: the local Anybody variable family. Width and weight change with lifecycle state; small interface copy remains sturdy and inspectable.
- Voice: precise, direct, imaginative, and quietly witty. Avoid vague AI superlatives, fake metrics, pseudo-technical decoration, defensive process copy, and template portfolio language.

Do not change the tagline wording without explicit approval. Real project names, owner identity, résumé, contact route, and production shipment remain pending until supplied.
