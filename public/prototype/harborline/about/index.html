About

Harborline started in 1987 as a two-man rigging shop on Dockside Lane. Nearly four decades later we run a full-service yard with a 35-ton travel lift, a certified engine bay, and a crew that averages fifteen years on the water.

We work on sail and power, wood and glass, weekend skiffs and offshore cruisers. If it floats on the Chesapeake, someone on our dock has fixed one like it.

Our promise is simple: honest estimates, straight timelines, and no surprise line items when you pick up the boat.
