Ecosystem finding: no dedicated 3D/WebGL agent skill exists in official or community sources. The spectacle-escalation rules in `art-direction.md` are original synthesis from this repository's own raw-WebGL2 precedent (fixed-geometry renderer with endpoint tests, zero-idle-frame law, SVG/DOM fallback contract).

Result: one mixed generate/apply/audit skill. It keeps domain-derived art direction and executable browser critique, while discarding style catalogs and stack-specific recipes.

## Cross-source conclusions

Award work is not united by WebGL, maximalism, or dark palettes. It is united by:

1. one ownable thesis applied across the whole experience;
2. content and interface sharing the same metaphor;
3. motion that changes meaning or position, not just opacity;
4. a signature available immediately on mobile and desktop;
5. distinct rhythms rather than repeated components;
6. craft that survives after the novelty passes;
7. an obvious path from wonder to proof.

AI-looking work converges toward statistically safe combinations. Avoiding one cliché is insufficient; the full cluster must be interrupted by subject-derived choices, real irregularity, and a design-specific browser loop.
